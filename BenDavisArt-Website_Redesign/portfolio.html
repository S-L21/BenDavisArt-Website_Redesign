Capitola Begonia festival poster

>

This poster, featuring the painting “If We Could Rewrite The Stars,” was commissioned by the Pajaro Valley Community Health Trust as the official poster for an Evening of Wine and Roses, organised in commemoration of their 20th aniversary.

The Young Guitarist

>

Painting Commission: This painting “The Young Guitarist” featuring Jimmy Hendricks was commissioned as part of the Santa Cruz Master’s, Picasso inspired, First Friday Art Show in 2019. This piece is from Davis’s Blues period and is based on the “Old Guitarist” from Picasso’s Blue Period.

Pinot Noir from the Santa Cruz Mountains

>

A 2017 Pinot Noir from the Santa Cruz Mountains, Regan Vineyard, produced and bottled by Bargetto Winery. This painting “If We Could ReWrite the Stars” was commissioned by the Pajaro Valley Community Health Trust as the official poster for an Evening of Wine and Roses, organised in commemoration of their 20th aniversary.

Wine Labels

>

Commemorative Wine Label collaborations: Photo of three bottles - (Left) A 2013 Cabernet Sauvignon from the Santa Clara Valley and Wiedeman Vineyard, produced and bottled by Sones Cellars. This painting, “Time and Relative Dimensions in Santa Cruz” hangs in Sones Cellars and depicts Beach Street by the BoardWalk, painted in 2014 to commemorate the Santa Cruz Wharf’s 100th anniversary. (Middle) A 2010 Cabernet Sauvignon from the Santa Clara Valley and Wiedeman Vineyard, produced and bottled by Sones Cellars. This painting “Once in a Blue Begonia Moon hangs in Sones Cellars tasting room and has been honored by being the official poster of the 61st Annual Capitola Begonia Festival in 2013. (Right) A 2012 Cabernet Sauvignon from the Santa Clara Valley and Wiedeman Vineyard, produced and bottled by Sones Cellars. This painting “Starship Capitola” also hangs in the Sones Cellars tasting room and was commissioned for the official poster of the Capitola Art and Wine Festival in 2016.
